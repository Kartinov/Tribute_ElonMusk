Musk's Biography

Early Life

Elon Reeve Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. His mother is Maye Musk, a model and dietitian born in Saskatchewan, Canada, but raised in South Africa. His father is Errol Musk, a South African electromechanical engineer, pilot, sailor, consultant, and property developer.

Personal Life

Musk met his first wife, Canadian author Justine Wilson, while attending Queen's University, and they married in 2000. Their first child, son Nevada Alexander Musk, died of sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS) at the age of 10 weeks. The couple divorced in 2008 and share custody of their five surviving children.
